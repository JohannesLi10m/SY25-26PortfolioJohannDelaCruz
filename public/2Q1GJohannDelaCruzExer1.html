Hypacrosaurus:

According to Jurassic Park Wiki (n.d.), the name Hypacrosaurus means "Near the Highest Lizard," chosen because of its size, which was almost as big as the Tyrannosaurus Rex. The Hypacrosaurus was a duck-billed dinosaur about 30 ft long and 13 ft tall, weighing around 4.4 tonnes.

According to The Children's Museum of Indianapolis (2021, August 2), the Hypacrosaurus was a herbivore based on fossil evidence and that it laid eggs.

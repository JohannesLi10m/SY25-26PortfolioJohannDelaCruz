Hypacrosaurus:

According to Jurassic Park Wiki(n.d) the name, Hypacrosaurus, means Near the highest lizard, the reason for this name was because of its size which is almost as big as the Tyrannosaurus Rex. The Hypacrosaurus was a duck-billed dinosaur at a length of 30 ft. and a height of 13 ft., while its weight was around 4.4 tonnes.

According to The Children's museum of Indianapolis(2021, August 2), the Hypacrosaurus was a Herbivore based from its fossils, and that it laid eggs
